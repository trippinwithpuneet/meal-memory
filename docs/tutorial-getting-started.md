# Getting Started with Meal Memory

By the end of this tutorial you will have an account, a household, your first recipe, and a full week of meals planned. It takes about 5 minutes.

---

## Before you begin

- Meal Memory runs on iOS 16+
- You need an internet connection for the initial setup
- Once your plan is loaded, offline browsing works

---

## Step 1 — Create an account

1. Open Meal Memory.
2. On the **Welcome** screen, tap **Create Account**.
3. Enter your email address and a password (minimum 8 characters).
4. Tap **Sign Up**.

You'll land on the **Household Setup** screen.

> **If you've been invited to join someone else's household**, skip to Step 2b — Join a Household instead.

---

## Step 2a — Create a household

1. Tap **Create a New Household**.
2. Enter a name (e.g. "Our Place" or your last name).
3. Tap **Create**.

You'll land on the **Plan** tab with an empty week grid. Your display name is automatically set from your email address — you can edit it on the Household tab.

---

## Step 2b — Join a household (alternate path)

If someone already has a household and gave you a 6-character invite code:

1. Tap **Join with Code** on the Household Setup screen.
2. Type the code exactly as shared.
3. Tap **Join**.

You'll land on the Plan tab with the household's existing data visible.

---

## Step 3 — Add your first recipe

1. Tap the **Recipes** tab (middle tab).
2. Tap **+** in the top right.
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Dal Tadka"
   - **Emoji** — tap the emoji field and pick something that represents the dish
   - **Ingredients** — tap **Add ingredient**, type each one, press Return
   - **Steps** — tap **Add step**, describe what to do
4. If the recipe suits any dietary restrictions, tap **Safe For** and toggle the relevant tags.
5. Tap **Save**.

The recipe appears in your Recipe Bank.

> **Shortcut:** if you have a URL to a recipe on a food blog, tap **Import from URL** at the top of the Add Recipe sheet. Name, ingredients, and steps auto-fill from the page.

---

## Step 4 — Place the recipe on the plan

1. Tap the **Plan** tab (calendar icon, first tab).
2. The week grid shows 3 meal rows (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner) across the days of the week. The grid scrolls horizontally and centers on today, so about four days are visible at once — swipe sideways to reach the rest.
3. Tap any empty cell (e.g. Monday Dinner).
4. A recipe picker slides up showing your Recipe Bank.
5. Tap **Dal Tadka** (or whatever you added).
6. The recipe name and emoji appear in the cell.

Repeat for as many slots as you like. Each slot updates instantly on both phones if a second person has joined your household.

---

## Step 5 — Navigate between weeks

- Swipe left on the grid (or tap the **›** arrow at the top right) to move to next week.
- Swipe right (or tap **‹**) to go back.
- Tap the **calendar icon** (between the arrows) to open a full month overview and see the dates around you.

---

## Step 6 — Set dietary restrictions (optional)

If anyone in your household has dietary restrictions:

1. Tap the **Household** tab (person icon, rightmost tab).
2. Tap your name in the member list.
3. Toggle on any restrictions that apply to you.
4. Tap **Save**.

Restrictions are saved per member and used as recipe-level labels. The week grid does not show a per-slot conflict dot — in a shared household the assumption is that you plan meals with each other's restrictions in mind. Recipes that need prep the night before show a 🌙 marker in the top-right of their slot instead; long-press a slot for the "Prep needed the night before" note.

---

## What's next

- **Invite your partner or housemate:** Household tab → **Invite Someone** → share the 6-char code → they create an account and join
- **Import more recipes from URLs:** Recipes → + → Import from URL
- **Enable weekly reminders:** Household → Reminders → toggle on Friday planning reminder
- **Archive recipes you no longer use:** Recipes → swipe left on a row → Archive
- **Stuck on what to cook?** Tap the **What can I cook?** pill at the bottom of the Plan tab to open **Fridge Raid** — type in the ingredients you have on hand and it ranks recipes you can make right now.

## Getting help

If something isn't working:
- Check that you have an internet connection (required for initial load)
- Check the Household tab to confirm you're in a household
- If the plan looks empty after a reconnect, pull down to refresh
