## Cross-cutting (hits multiple pages)

- **[Major][UX] No "jump to today / this week" on the Plan page.** Once you navigate away from the current week there's no one-tap return. The month calendar's "Today" button only resets which month you're *browsing* — it does not move the plan grid. Add a "Today"/"This week" affordance on the Plan page itself.
- **[Minor][UI] Single-letter weekday labels are ambiguous.** Both the week grid (`WeekGridView.shortDayName` → `prefix(1)`) and the month calendar (`MonthCalendarView.dayLabels`) render `M T W T F S S` — two T's and two S's, impossible to tell apart at a glance. Consider `Mon`-style two-letter labels, or pair the weekday letter with the date number.

## Plan page — `WeekGridView` / `PlanTabView`

- **[Major][UX] Day columns show no date number.** The header is just the weekday letter; you can't tell *which date* a column is without opening the month view. Add the day-of-month number under the weekday letter (e.g. `M / 30`).
- **[Major][UX] Calendar can't actually jump the plan.** See the MonthCalendarView section — tapping a day is a no-op, so the calendar icon promises navigation it doesn't deliver. (The tutorial now claims you can "jump to any week" — fix the code to match, or the doc is wrong.)
- **[Minor][UI] Empty-slot "+" is very low contrast.** `SlotCell` draws the `+` in `Theme.border`, which is near-invisible on the empty-cell background. Bump to `Theme.textTertiary` or saffron at low opacity so empty slots read as tappable.
- **[Minor][UI] Header crowding on 375 pt.** When the week title becomes a date range ("Jun 30 – Jul 6") it shares one row with Add + Share + ‹ 📅 ›. `minimumScaleFactor(0.7)` will shrink the title rather than truncate, but verify it doesn't get tiny on the mini; if so, drop Share to an overflow.
- **[Minor][UX] Vertical fit is tight on the mini.** Header + 3 rows (96 pt each) + the bottom hero pill + tab bar + safe areas. Confirm nothing clips at the bottom and the hero pill never overlaps the home indicator.
- **[Minor][UX] Hero pill ↔ trash panel swap.** Selecting a filled slot hides the "What can I cook?" pill and slides up the trash/view panel in the same spot. Confirm the transition reads cleanly and doesn't flicker.

## Month calendar — `MonthCalendarView`

- **[Major][UX] Tapping a day does nothing.** `CalendarDayCell` has no tap handler and `MonthCalendarView` takes no callback / binding to `viewModel.weekStart`. The view is browse-only. To make the calendar icon useful, pass a `onSelectDate` (or bind weekStart), have the day cell call it, then dismiss and scroll the plan to that week.
- **[Minor][UI] Reserved cycle-tracking dots add dead height.** Each day cell reserves 3 clear dots (~13 pt) for a future feature. On the mini this pushes the grid + teaser taller; confirm the 6-row month + "Cycle tracking — Soon" teaser don't crowd. Consider hiding the reserved row until the feature ships.
- **[Minor][UX] "Cycle tracking — Soon" teaser is permanent.** A persistent "coming soon" card spends prime space on something users can't use. Fine as a one-time tease; reconsider making it dismissible.

## Recipes — `RecipeBankView` / `RecipeRowView`

- **[Minor][UX] Archive has no undo.** Swipe → row disappears immediately (`archiveRecipe`), no toast / undo. Easy to archive the wrong recipe. Add an undo snackbar or a confirm.
- **[Minor][UI] Archive swipe uses destructive role but gray tint.** `role: .destructive` + `.tint(Theme.textTertiary)` sends mixed signals (red intent, gray color). Archive isn't deletion — drop the destructive role so it doesn't read as dangerous.
- **[Minor][UI] Double card padding.** `RecipeRowView` is a padded card, nested in a `List` row that also insets. Looks fine but verify the row height isn't bulky on the mini.

## Household — `HouseholdView` / `MemberEditSheet`

- **[Minor][UI] Sign out / Delete account section has no header.** Every other section has a colored header; this one is bare buttons. Add a header (e.g. "Account") for rhythm.
- **[Minor][UX] Restriction taxonomy overlaps.** `allRestrictions` lists both "No milk" and "Dairy-free", plus "Vegan"/"Vegetarian"/"Jain". The milk/dairy overlap is confusing — collapse to one term.
- **[Minor][UI] Member edit sheet detent may be too short.** `MemberEditSheet` is `.presentationDetents([.medium])` with a name field + 7 restriction rows. On the mini, medium likely can't show all 7 without scrolling. Consider `[.medium, .large]`.

## Auth — `AuthView`

- **[Major][UX] Raw error strings.** Errors surface `error.localizedDescription` straight from Supabase ("Invalid login credentials", network dumps). Map the common cases to friendly copy.
- **[Minor][UX] No password helpers.** No reveal toggle, no min-length hint on sign-up, no forgot-password path. At minimum add a sign-up length hint so account creation doesn't fail silently.
- **[Minor][UI] Keyboard-avoidance on the mini.** The form is centered with `Spacer()`s and no `ScrollView`. Verify the keyboard lifts the password field + button into view on the mini and doesn't cover the submit button.
